Our Mission

At WyldPatch, we're on a mission to transform sterile lawns into thriving native habitats, restore damaged forests, and create sanctuaries for birds and wildlife. We believe every yard can be a patch of wild nature.

Transform Lawns

Convert grass monocultures into beautiful, biodiverse native landscapes

Create habitats and food sources for birds and native animals

Join the WyldPatch Movement

Every native plant, every restored yard, every forest patch contributes to a healthier ecosystem. Together, we can create a network of wild spaces that support life and bring nature back to our communities.
